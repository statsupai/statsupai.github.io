The Spatial Transcriptomics Data

This online resource aims to provide researchers, academics, and enthusiasts with key overviews and direct access to spatial transcriptomics data. The center compiles multiple important datasets, fostering collaboration, enhancing data transparency, and advancing scientific progress in the field of spatial transcriptomics.

Name Description Website
Spatial Transcriptomics Arena(STAr) The Spatial Transcriptomics Arena(STAr) is an open platform for methodology research. It provides resources including curated datasets from simulation and real studies (Dataset tab), accessible analysis methods (Method tab), and reproducible analysis results (Analysis tab). It also welcomes new datasets, methods, or analysis results contributed by the broad research community. (600+ Simulation Datasets, 193+ Real Datasets, 5 Spatial Patterns, 4 Sparsity Settings.) STAr
STimage-1K4M The STimage-1K4M dataset contains 1,149 spatial transcriptomics slides, with over 4 million spots paired with gene expression data. It is designed to foster research in spatial transcriptomics by combining high-resolution histopathology images with gene expression data and spatial coordinates for each spot. STimage-1K4M
